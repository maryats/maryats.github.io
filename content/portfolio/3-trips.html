--- title: "3 Trips Visualization" date: 2018-11-12T20:31:59-05:00 draft: false tags: ["design", "information design", "brochure"] ---

This piece presents information about three trips: from my apartment to a classroom, from my apartment to my parents' home in New York, and from New York to Moscow.

This piece was created for the information design class at Northeastern University, and is designed to be viewed as an accordion with 7 panels.
