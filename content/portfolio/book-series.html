These book covers are for a hypothetical series about three influential architects. I designed them for the Typography II course at Northeastern University.

I wanted to showcase the unique aspects of each architect's works, and for this I drew inspiration from the architects' drafting sketches, which give an fascinating glimpse into their thought process.
