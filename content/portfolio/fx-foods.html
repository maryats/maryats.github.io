In Fall 2018, I worked as a designer and developer for Scout, Northeastern University's student-led design studio. Our five-person team (three designers, two developers) created a new brand identity and assets, illustrations, packaging, a brand book, and a fully-functional marketing and e-commerce website in three months.

I worked primarily on user experience design aspects, helping create lo-fi packaging and website wireframes in addition to conducting user research and iterating on logo designs.

In the second half of the semester, I worked with the other studio developer to translate high-fidelity mocks into a responsive Wordpress website. We used Twig to create a custom Wordpress theme, and used Woocommerce for the e-commerce backend. This was my first time using PHP.
