This piece was inspired by the movement of starling murmurations.

Process

To get inspired, I watched this video on repeat and sketched for an hour.

Then, I carved my favorite sketch into floral foam.

I used a 3D scanner to digitize the sculpture, then sliced it into 52 pieces for the laser cutter using SketchUp.
