Spooky FM Video Game

During the 2017 Global Game Jam, I worked on a team with six other students to build a video game from scratch in 48 hours.

We created Spooky FM, a 2D side-scrolling survival game where you play as a radio DJ who tries to appease an influx of monsters by playing their favorite music. Battle your station’s awful WiFI connection and your own caffeine dependency while making sure to fulfill audience requests to keep the ratings up!

This project was my first foray into sprite animations and pixel art, and I created the sprites and animations for the player character and monsters. I also worked with the other artists on the team to develop a consistent visual style (lineless pixel art, consistent sprite scale, color palette).
