Spooky FM Video Game

As part of the 2017 Global Game Jam held at Northeastern University, I worked with on a team with six other students to build a game from scratch in 48 hours.

Our team created Spooky FM, a 2D side-scrolling survival game where you play as a radio DJ who changes their station’s music to appease an ongoing stream of monsters by playing their favorite music, take audience requests, and keep the ratings up! You also battle your station’s awful WiFI connection, and your own caffeine dependency.

I created the player character sprite, as well as monster sprites and splash screens. This was my first foray into sprite animations as well as detailed pixel art. I also helped coordinate the art team’s efforts in developing a consistent visual style (lineless pixel art, consistent sprite scale, color palette).
