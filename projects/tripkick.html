TripKick Browser Extension Concept

TripKick is designed to be a trip-planning travel sidekick.

Sparked by user research that found that in most families, mothers are the ones doing the lion’s share of trip-planning, TripKick aims to streamline the trip-planning process through a web extension that helps organize information and create an itinerary.

During the trip itself, the TripKick mobile app tracks discounts and deals, provides optimized routes, and suggests back-up plans to help take the stress out of traveling.
